Pick the languages you speak and the one you want to learn. WikiLinkua finds the words whose meanings cross over and turns them into a flashcard quiz with pronunciation audio — so you start from vocabulary you already almost know.

A quiz on cognates vs faux amis. Two similar-looking words from two languages — do they mean the same thing, or not? Mixes famous false-friend pairs, Wikidata P5976 statements, and computed true friends.
