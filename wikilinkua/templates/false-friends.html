How this works. The quiz mixes three data sources: a hand-curated list of famous false-friend pairs in well-studied language couples, explicit P5976 (false friend) sense statements on Wikidata (~190 globally, sense-level), and "true-friend" pairs derived from P5137 (item for this sense) bridge words filtered to lemmas with high orthographic similarity (Levenshtein ≥ 55%) so they look genuinely confusing. The cross-script Hindi/Urdu pair is included as a register-drift example, not a true false friend.
