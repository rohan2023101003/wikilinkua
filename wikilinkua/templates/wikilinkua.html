How this works. A "bridge word" is a target-language Wikidata Lexeme whose sense is linked (via P5137) to the same concept as a sense in one of your known languages. The 🌉 badge marks pairs connected by a chain of P5191 ("derived from lexeme") — cognates and loanwords (e.g. Indonesian kabar ← Arabic خبر). The 🔊 badge marks pairs whose P898 IPA strings have Levenshtein similarity ≥ 60% — words that sound alike even if their etymologies aren't recorded yet. Audio on flashcards is pulled from P443. The "%" is bridges divided by the total count of target-language lexemes that have any P5137-linked sense.
